Answers to your questions

Please review the following frequently asked questions, if you can't find the answer you're looking for navigate to our contact page and send us an email with your inquiry.

1How do I schedule an appointment?

Please click on the “Schedule Appointment” Icon on our Home Page and follow our easy scheduling process.

2Safety Protocols?

At Four Brothers, we have the best safety procedures and policies implemented. Not only does Four Brothers have qualified technicians, but only premium tools, equipment and products are used during service calls. The car technician will use a portable safety barrier to ensure no one comes within proximity of the car, as well as secure car jack to lift the vehicle if necessary. Also, our technicians use a heavy duty mat underneath the vehicle to prevent any fluid leaks.

3What are Four Brothers’ Credentials?

All of our technicians are certified through an designated apprenticeship and many years of experience, and have worked with some of the most well-known Auto Service shops in Canada

4Products Used?

We ensure that all our products and equipment are high-quality, durable, and positively recognized amongst the Auto Service Industry.

5 Payment Process?

Payment will be collected at the end of scheduling your appointment through our on-line secure merchant Stripe. All payments are secure and are in accordance with privacy laws and regulations. Your payment will include taxes and gratuities. Four Brothers does NOT store your credit card data.

6Do I have to be home while the technician is serving the vehicle?

We offer a service that includes servicing your vehicle when you are not at home. For example, for services such as an oil change, we just require that you leave your hood slightly open so that we can gain access to your car. Once we are finished, we make sure that your vehicle hood is securely closed.

7Additional services on-site after the technician arrives?

If you require additional auto services on-site, you will be able to pay the technician on-site for select services.

8How does the scheduling an appointment work?

When choosing an appointment, we will initially give you three (3) time-slots to choose from. The night before your scheduled appointment, we will send you another email giving you a more precise time for your appointment, which will usually be 1.5 hours in duration.

9How does same day service work?

We unfortunately cannot accommodate same-day requests. We require that all our appointments be scheduled at least the day before your requested scheduled date. As such, we will not be able to service your car if your appointment is requested the same day due to due to prior commitments to other Four Brothers’ customers.

10How does the cancellation policy work?

All cancellations must be made within 24 hours of scheduling your appointment. There will no charge for appointments that are cancelled within 24 hours. After 24 hours, you will not be able to cancel your appointment; however, you can reschedule your appointment to a future date.

+More FAQ

xi. How do I change my requested services?

You are allowed to change your requested services and/or products up to 24 hours before your scheduled appointment by accessing your profile account. Unfortunately, you will not be allowed to change your requested services the same day as your scheduled appointment with some exceptions. Once the technician arrives, you can request a change of services and/or for additional services to be completed. The reasoning behind this is that our technicians have pre-determined what supplies and equipment they need and may not be able to obtain the necessary supplies to fulfill your service change request.

xii. How does rescheduling my appointment work?

If you choose to reschedule your appointment, you can simply sign-in to your account/profile and choose another date. Unfortunately, you will not be permitted to change your scheduled appointment the same day as your appointment. xiii. What happens if I miss my appointment or fail to leave my hood open? If you miss a scheduled appointment or forget to open the hood of your car, please change your scheduled appointment as soon as possible. Once we arrive, we will call the given number multiple times and wait 10 minutes. If you do not arrive within that 10 minute period, we will be forced to leave for our next scheduled appointment. As such, you will be charged a missed appointment fee which is the lesser of your appointment cost or $49.99.

xiv. What is the minimum appointment cost?

In order to effectively deliver your auto service requests, we require that your schedule appointment meet a minimum charge of $39.99.

xv. What areas do Four Brothers service?

We service the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) and the area just north of Toronto. When you schedule your appointment, you will be able to see our permitted delivery areas (Map is also shown on Serviceable Area Map provided). If you are just outside our permitted area, we will still delivery to your address at an additional $4.99 delivery charge. If you are outside our permitted delivery area by a large distance, we will unfortunately not be able to provide our services to you at this time.

xvi. Money-Back Guarantee:

Four Brothers has a 100% money-back guarantee. As a company that prides itself as being consumer friendly and always making sure to put the consumer’s needs first, Four Brothers have implemented a 100% money-back guarantee. If a customer is not 100% satisfied with our service and Four Brothers is unable to fix the issues, we will provide a full refund within 5 business days of the completed scheduled appointment. All inquirers must be made within 48 hours of the completed scheduled appointment in order for Four Brothers to properly assess the issue at hand.

xvii. Is Four Brothers fully insured?

We are fully insured up to $2,000,000.00, which includes commercial car insurance and business liability insurance. We also have our technicians fully covered by WSIB.

xviii. Does Four Brothers service larger vehicles?

We are currently only equipped to service light-duty vehicles. Unfortunately we cannot service vehicles larger than 3 tons.

xix. Does Four Brothers recycle?

We are eco-friendly. All products are thereby recycled in an environmentally friendly manner to dispose of all of our used oil & other used products in accordance with the City of Toronto Waste Management Hazardous Materials Policy Code.

xx. What are the hours of operations?

Please see our Hours of Operations below – link that sends you to the hours of operations. We do not work on statutory holidays in accordance to the law.

xxi. Four Brothers requesting a time change:

In rare circumstances, such as unforeseen weather condition or emergency situation, Four Brothers may ask the customer to change their requested time slot to a different time on the same day or the following day.
