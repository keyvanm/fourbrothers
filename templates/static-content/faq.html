xi. How do I change my requested services? You are allowed to change your requested services and/or products up to 24 hours before your scheduled appointment by accessing your profile account. Unfortunately, you will not be allowed to change your requested services the same day as your scheduled appointment with some exceptions. Once the technician arrives, you can request a change of services and/or for additional services to be completed. The reasoning behind this is that our technicians have pre-determined what supplies and equipment they need and may not be able to obtain the necessary supplies to fulfill your service change request. xii. How does rescheduling my appointment work? If you choose to reschedule your appointment, you can simply sign-in to your account/profile and choose another date. Unfortunately, you will not be permitted to change your scheduled appointment the same day as your appointment. xiii. What happens if I miss my appointment or fail to leave my hood open? If you miss a scheduled appointment or forget to open the hood of your car, please change your scheduled appointment as soon as possible. Once we arrive, we will call the given number multiple times and wait 10 minutes. If you do not arrive within that 10 minute period, we will be forced to leave for our next scheduled appointment. As such, you will be charged a missed appointment fee which is the lesser of your appointment cost or $49.99. xiv. What is the minimum appointment cost? In order to effectively deliver your auto service requests, we require that your schedule appointment meet a minimum charge of $39.99. xv. What areas do Four Brothers service? We service the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) and the area just north of Toronto. When you schedule your appointment, you will be able to see our permitted delivery areas (Map is also shown on Serviceable Area Map provided). If you are just outside our permitted area, we will still delivery to your address at an additional $4.99 delivery charge. If you are outside our permitted delivery area by a large distance, we will unfortunately not be able to provide our services to you at this time. xvi. Money-Back Guarantee: Four Brothers has a 100% money-back guarantee. As a company that prides itself as being consumer friendly and always making sure to put the consumer’s needs first, Four Brothers have implemented a 100% money-back guarantee. If a customer is not 100% satisfied with our service and Four Brothers is unable to fix the issues, we will provide a full refund within 5 business days of the completed scheduled appointment. All inquirers must be made within 48 hours of the completed scheduled appointment in order for Four Brothers to properly assess the issue at hand. xvii. Is Four Brothers fully insured? We are fully insured up to $2,000,000.00, which includes commercial car insurance and business liability insurance. We also have our technicians fully covered by WSIB. xviii. Does Four Brothers service larger vehicles? We are currently only equipped to service light-duty vehicles. Unfortunately we cannot service vehicles larger than 3 tons. xix. Does Four Brothers recycle? We are eco-friendly. All products are thereby recycled in an environmentally friendly manner to dispose of all of our used oil & other used products in accordance with the City of Toronto Waste Management Hazardous Materials Policy Code. xx. What are the hours of operations? Please see our Hours of Operations below – link that sends you to the hours of operations. We do not work on statutory holidays in accordance to the law. xxi. Four Brothers requesting a time change: In rare circumstances, such as unforeseen weather condition or emergency situation, Four Brothers may ask the customer to change their requested time slot to a different time on the same day or the following day.
