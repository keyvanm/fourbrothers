WARRANTY APPROVED AND FULLY COVERED

Four Brothers is a fully insured and bondable auto service company. This includes liability insurance of up to $2,000,000 and WSIB for all of our certifiable and licensed technicians. Not only are we full covered, but we guarantee that your vehicle’s warranty will be in the same condition. Besides from taking the upmost care of your car, we use leading products in the Auto Service industry to keep your vehicle running at its full capacity. At Four Brothers, we make sure to keep record of all of your vehicle’s previous service appointments with us, thereby ensuring that we are as up-to-date as possible. Four Brothers is also a fully licensed garage with permits allowing us to service vehicles. At Four Brothers, we make sure that we leave nothing to chance and make it our top priority to fulfill all commitments to our loyal customers.

ENVIRONMENTALLY CONSCIOUS

At Four Brothers, we make sure we are environmentally conscious every step of the way. While we conduct our vehicle auto services, we strive to use products that are recyclable and environmentally friendly. We place a safety mat underneath the vehicle so that not a single drop of our lubricants leak onto the ground. We guarantee that there will be no spills and that the drive-way/garage will be left in the same condition. During disposal, none of our oil products are shipped to landfill sites. We make sure that all of our used oil is given to a responsible waste management company who specializes in re-using our products for other industrial products and lubricants.
