What We're All About

Innovation. Convenience. Efficiency.

These are three things that Four Brothers Auto Service believes has been overlooked in the Auto Service Industry. Mobile auto service allows customers to receive services without ever having to leave the comfort of their home or office.

Four Brothers Auto Service now offers our customers the option to have their vehicle serviced while they are not home! All the customer needs to do is have their vehicle hood slightly open for oil changes or spare tires accessible for tire services.

Warranty Approved & Fully Covered

Four Brothers Auto Service is a fully insured and bondable auto service company. This includes liability insurance of up to $2,000,000 and WSIB for all of our certifiable and licensed technicians.

Not only are we full covered, but we guarantee that your vehicle’s warranty will be in the same condition. Besides from taking the upmost care of your car, we use leading products in the Auto Service industry to keep your vehicle running at its full capacity. At Four Brothers Auto Service, we make sure to keep record of all of your vehicle’s previous service appointments with us, helping us keep as up-to-date as possible. We leave nothing to chance and make it our top priority to fulfill all commitments to our loyal customers.

Environmentally Conscious

While we conduct our vehicle auto services, we strive to use products that are recyclable and environmentally friendly. We place a safety mat underneath the vehicle so that not a single drop of oil leaks onto the ground. As such, we guarantee that there will be no spills or residue leftover.

During disposal, none of our oil products are shipped to landfill sites. We make sure that all of our used oil, filters and used products are given to a reputable waste management company, Safety Kleen, who specializes in re-using our products for other industrial products and lubricants.
