Corporate Information

Four Brothers Auto Service is a customer-friendly mobile auto service company. With the hard work and dedication of a committed staff, we are able to offer a premium service at an affordable rate.

CEO/Founder | Adam Zameret

Adam Zameret is the Founder and CEO of Four Brothers. Before establishing Four Brothers, Adam has had previous experience in entrepreneurship, growing up in a family business, as well as operating a successful logistics company for the Province of Ontario. Following his passion for entrepreneurship, Adam decided to go back to school to get his MBA at Schulich School of Business in Entrepreneurship and Finance. Growing up in Toronto, Adam has been able to identify the inconvenience of typical garages and the need for a more convenient auto service company with a stronger consumer-orientated business approach.

CTO/Partner | Jeffrey Gingras

Jeffrey Gingras is the CTO of Four Brothers. His web development and design agency SAWC has built the Four Brothers web application. His team specializes in frontend and backend web application development. Jeff has a passion for tech startups and is a dedicated entrepreneur. He graduated with an Honours Bachelor of Science from the University of Toronto in 2012.
